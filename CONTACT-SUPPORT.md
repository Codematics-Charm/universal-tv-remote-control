# Contact Support

Need help with Universal TV Remote Control?

Please contact Codematics support through the official website or app store support links.

contact@codematics.co
support@codematics.co

## Before Contacting Support

Please include:

- Your TV brand
- Your TV model if available
- Your phone model
- Android or iOS version
- Connection type, WiFi or IR
- Short description of the issue

This helps the support team understand and solve the issue faster.
