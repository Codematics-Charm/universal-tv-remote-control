# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Universal TV Remote Control?

Universal TV Remote Control is a remote control app by Codematics that helps users control Smart TVs and supported TVs through WiFi or IR connectivity.

## Does the app support WiFi remote control?

Yes. Universal TV Remote Control supports WiFi connectivity for compatible Smart TVs.

## Does the app support IR remote control?

Yes. IR connectivity is supported if your phone has a built in IR blaster.

## How many TV brands are supported?

Universal TV Remote Control supports 700 plus TV brands.

## Is the app available worldwide?

Yes. The app is used in more than 100 countries.

## Where can I download the app?

You can download Universal TV Remote Control from Google Play and the Apple App Store.
