# Troubleshooting Guide

This guide helps fix common issues with Universal TV Remote Control.

## App Finds My TV But Buttons Do Not Work

Make sure your phone and TV are connected to the same WiFi network. Restart the TV, restart the app, and pair the TV again.

## TV Not Showing in the App

Check that your Smart TV is turned on and connected to the same WiFi network as your phone. Also make sure mobile data or VPN is not interfering with local network discovery.

## IR Remote Not Working

Make sure your phone has an IR blaster. If your phone does not support IR, use WiFi mode instead.

## Voice Control Not Working

Check microphone permission in your phone settings. Also make sure your TV model supports voice related control features.

## Keyboard Not Working

Some TVs require permission before keyboard input works. Reconnect the TV and accept any pairing or permission message on your TV screen.
